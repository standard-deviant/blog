7 tips on naming your dev tool

1. Maximum 3 syllables, fewer the better

2. Use food words

especially dryfruits, spices, dessert, and coffee drinks mint pista mocha latte basil chilli eclair honey vanilla pepper gelato wasabi chestnut espresso cinnamon spearmint

3. Use color words

red ruby rust lime blue azure amber olive chrome lumen chromatic indigo iridescent

4. Use science words

especially from chemistry ice acid atom neon oxide proton phospor kelvin crystal electron kinetic isotope

5. Use English words of Japanese origin

high-vowel content makes them easier to remember zen sumo judo dojo ninja kendo emoji bento sensei samurai kaizen

6. Verbs make good prefixes/suffixes

7. Combine words

with atleast one word connected to your project's functionality pin-ninja (identify all UART pins with a single script)
nimblebox (out-of-the-box cloud environments)
Wafer (migration tool for AWS WAF)
Hope this helps! Thanks to X & Y for review.
