7 tips on naming your dev tool

1. maximum 3 syllables, lesser the better

2. Use food words (especially dryfruits, spices, dessert, and coffee drinks)

mint pista mocha latte basil chilli eclair honey vanilla pepper gelato wasabi chestnut espresso cinnamon spearmint

3. Use chemistry words

ice acid atom neon oxide proton phospor kelvin crystal electron kinetic isotope

4. Use color & light words

red ruby rust lime blue azure amber olive chrome lumen chromatic indigo iridescent

5. Use English words of Japanese origin

(high-vowel content makes them easier to remember) zen sumo judo dojo ninja kendo emoji bento sensei samurai kaizen

6. Verbs make good prefixes/suffixes

7. Combine words (with atleast one word related to your project)

pin-ninja (identify all UART pins with a single script) nimblebox (out-of-the-box cloud environments) Wafer (migration tool for AWS WAF) Hope this helps! Thanks to X & Y for review.
